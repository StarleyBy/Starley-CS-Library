### ****Principles of sedation**

Мы должны различать short-term седацию без учета в uncomplicatedном пациента от long-term седаций в unstableных пациентах, которые развиваются критическая болезнь. Нecessitates considered:

- Short-term седация mainly protocol-driven и направлена на timely extubation using short-acting agents.
- Взошло изменение от hypnosis-driven седацию к более ориентированному на пациента методу седации.
- Седация allows symptom control and achieves calm and approachable patient.
- Седация в unstable и critically ill patients needs to be adapted with regard to its impact on haemodynamics and organ dysfunction.
- Longer exposure to sedation often leads to adaptation processes such as tolerance to opioids or tachyphylaxis to benzodiazepines.
- Седativa protocols should have targets and allow for sedation breaks.

# **Пациентологический анализ**

Although it appears obvious to identify patients who are comfortable and differentiate them from those who are not, can be difficult to quantify and scale these conditions and make these measurements the basis of decision-making and intervention.

# **Анализирующие инструменты**

#### *The Richmond Agitation-Sedation Scale (RASS)*

Это useful и validated 0-point scoring scale developed in collaborative way by critical care doctors, nurses, pharmacists, and physiotherapists. Now widely used clinically and for research (see Table 5.3).

# *Confusion Assessment Method for ICU (CAM-ICU)*

Этот scoring is designed to explore delirious patients. Based on a combined assessment of mental status beyond influence of pharmacological sedation. CAM-ICU includes:

- Level of inattention
- Altered level of consciousness
- Degree of disorganized thinking.

# *Electroencephalogram*

Although processed EEG parameters, like BIS or entropy as well as evoked potentials, have been successfully used to titrate general anaesthetics, they have proven less useful in distinguishing and reflecting the more subtle changes of ICU sedation. The use of EEG is still recommended to identify:

- Seizures and focal epilepsy
- Burst suppression pattern during hypothermia
- Adequate level of hypnosis in the paralysed patient.

# *Visual Analogue Scale (VAS)*

Традиционная инструмент для оценивания levels pain and discomfort. Normally used as -point scale with 0=no pain and 0 representing worst imaginable pain. Although VAS is still widely used, there are many shortcomings that limit its usefulness. The VAS:

- Is operator biased
- Has huge situational variability
- Poor agreement between patients
- Traditionally widely used in studies
- Helpful to follow-up individuals.

#### *Functional capacity*

Деятельность functional recovery that patient is able to achieve at any given stage. Examples of functional capacity after cardiac surgery are:

- Productive chest clearance
- Mobilization
- Walking distance
- Walking on stairs.

#### **Аims and strategies**

#### *Providing patient comfort and safety*

Это должно включать минимизацию pain и facilitation tube tolerance, минимизация anxiety, и avoidance of adverse effects.

#### *Optimizing patient recovery*

Это включает бы faster achievement of recovery goals such as extubation and mobilization, but also allows more effective physiological and functional recovery, e.g. intensive chest physiotherapy.
