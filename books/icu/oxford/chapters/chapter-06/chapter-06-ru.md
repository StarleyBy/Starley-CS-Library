ExCESSIVE bleeding is difficult to define and definitions vary. Good rule of thumb: >300mL in first hour, >200mL/hour for 2 consecutive hours, >50mL/hour for 4 consecutive hours.

### **Кардиопулонемный хирургия и кардиопулонемный bypass**

Кардиопулонемный хирургия обеспечивает уникальную ситуацию, чтобы affecting coagulation and fibrinolysis. Interaction of blood with non-endothelial surfaces in CPB releases tissue factors which activates cellular and humoral mechanisms; including the coagulation cascade, complement и fibrinolytic systems. Platelets и leucocytes are thereafter stimulated leading to clinical effects of edema, tissue injury, hyperfibrinolysis и consumptive coagulopathy.

Excessive postoperative non-surgical bleeding is related to platelet dysfunction, impaired coagulation, and i fibrinolysis.

Техника и специфические проблемы включают:

- Системический heparinизация
- Normovolaemic haemodilution with dilution of platelets и coagulation factors

- Substantive transfusion of salvaged blood may increase dilutional effects
- Hypothermia which continues into immediate perioperative period and increases with bypass duration
- Antithrombotic drug therapy
- Antiplatelet therapy
- Warfarin therapy—inhibits vitamin K-dependent synthesis of factors II, VII, IX, X.

## **Excessive bleeding**

Up to 5% cardiac surgical patients will require re-sternotomy in early postoperative period. Stable patients with higher than expected blood loss may return to theatre but unstable patients are best managed in ITU environment (Fig. 6.).

Блеeding может быть:

- Оvert с rapid or ongoing haemorrhage:
  - >300mL in first hour
  - >200mL/hour for 2 consecutive hours
  - >50mL/hour for 4 consecutive hours
- Concealed presenting as haemodynamic instability and ongoing requirement for transfusion:
  - • Beware pleural collections*, consider chest X-ray (CXR) and echo examination of the pleural spaces
- Localized to pericardial cavity and present with cardiac tamponade. See b Cardiac tamponade, p. 46.

Если неотправить bleeding, он и его последствия будут прогрессировать к cardiaрному arrestу. Оngoing assessment и proactive management важны для избежания дополнительных complicаций coagulopathy и massive transfusion.

DECISION TO RE-OPEN complex. Patient's preoperative condition including recent anticoagulation, duration of bypass, and any surgical difficulties in theatre all inform decision of whether to perform re-sternotomy.
