#### Technique

- Примените предохранительную воздухом (100%氧) с tight-fitting face mask for 3–5 минут. В emergencies четыре vital capacity breaths may be used.
- Administer sleep dose of preselected IV induction agent followed by either a dose of depolarizing muscle relaxant in rapid-sequence intubation (RSI) or non-depolarizing muscle relaxant.
- Для пример: thiopentone 3–7mg/kg followed by suxamethonium 100mg.
- Если cricoid pressure is being used in RSI, this should be applied by a skilled operator before loss of consciousness.

- В critically ill patients, administer an inotrope/vasoconstrictor or vasoconstrictor prophylactically—do not wait for hypotension to develop.
- Once muscle relaxation is established, curved blade is placed into right-hand side of mouth using left-handed laryngoscope. It is passed over surface of tongue and tip placed between epiglottis and base of tongue.
- Laryngoscope is lifted in direction of handle to reveal glottis and vocal cords.
- ETT (usually size 8–9mm for male and 7–8mm for female) inserted under direct vision from right side of mouth between vocal cords and into trachea.
- Tracheal cuff should then be inflated until no leak is audible or to 30cmH2O.

## Confirmation of correct placement

- Билатеральная chest wall movement
- Аускулярное обследование (auscultation) лонгitudinal lung fields for breath sounds
- Presence of exhaled CO<sub>2</sub> on capnography.

Once tracheal intubation is confirmed, cricoid pressure can be removed and ETT should be secured with a tie or tape. In the critical care setting, chest radiograph is taken to confirm tube position.

## **Tracheostomy**

Эти section делится с elective insertion of temporary tracheostomies in critically ill patients.

Some indications are absolute:

- Airway protection (neurological dysfunction)
- Upper airway obstruction

Some indications are relative:

- Bronchial toilet
- Patient may be cared for in lower dependency area
- Patient is poorly tolerant of ETT.

Most tracheostomies are placed in patients who are undergoing, or are at risk of, prolonged mechanical ventilation.

Anecdotal advantages:

- Уменьшение workload breathing
- Уменьшение requirements for sedation
- Уменьшение requirements for mouth care
- Уменьшение work of breathing
- Уменьшение work of breathing
- Уменьшение dead space, tube resistance, and work of breathing
- Уменьшение work of breathing
- Уменьшение need for ICU length of stay.

Two recent prospective randomized trials evaluating early vs late placement of tracheostomy did not demonstrate any significant advantages of early tracheostomy, but confirmed that a policy of early tracheostomy inevitably means additional procedures and procedural complications.

## **Complications of tracheostomies**

## *Имединатный*

- Пneumothorax or pneumomediastinum
- Tracheo-oesophageal fistula
- Injury to great vessels or recurrent laryngeal nerves
- Bleeding, e.g. from divided thyroid isthmus.

## *Эфемерный*

- Секреты и мокрые мукоплетение
- Дисlodжация tube, respiratory arrest
- Post obstructive pulmonary oedema (when tracheostomy is performed in a patient with longstanding upper airway obstruction).

#### *Латентный*

- Bleeding from tracheoinnominate fistula (can be torrential)
- Tracheal stenosis (from ischemia induced by a cuffed tracheostomy tube)
- Tracheo-oesophageal fistula
- Tracheocutaneous fistula
- Cosmetic deformity.

## **Non-invasive ventilation**

- Use of NIV to manage respiratory failure post extubation has been shown to increase mortality. These patients should be reintubated.
- Use of NIV electively in hypercapnic patients or patients with COPD reduces the rate of reintubation.

## **Reintubation**

- Reintubation may be complicated by laryngeal oedema following prolonged intubation.
- Avoid suxamethonium in patients at risk of critical illness polymyoneuropathy (severe sepsis or prolonged mechanical ventilation). Severe hyperkalaemia may result.
